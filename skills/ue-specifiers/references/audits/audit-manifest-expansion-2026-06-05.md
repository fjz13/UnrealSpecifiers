
- discovers normalized source records through `build_index.discover_records()`;
- includes only `kind=specifier` and `kind=meta`;
- preserves existing `status`, `priority`, and `finding_path` for already tracked IDs;
- assigns priorities for newly discovered rows using deterministic category/name rules;
- keeps `verified_UE5.8` evidence requirements unchanged.
